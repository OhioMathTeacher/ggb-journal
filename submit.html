For Authors

Submit a Manuscript

NAGJ accepts manuscripts on a rolling basis, year-round. No submission fees. Authors retain copyright.

ℹ To submit, use the contact form and attach your manuscript, or see the author guidelines below.

Author Guidelines

Manuscripts should be a maximum of 3,000 words. Use APA style for citations. Original figures, tables, and graphs should be embedded in the document — do not use text boxes, footnotes, or endnotes.

Submissions in LaTeX (.tex) are preferred. Microsoft Word or Plain Text are also accepted. Overleaf users may start from the official NAGJ template on Overleaf.

No cover sheet is needed — author information is entered during registration. No author identification should appear in the manuscript or its metadata.

Submission Checklist

  • Manuscript has not been previously published, nor is it under consideration elsewhere
  • File is in LaTeX (.tex), Microsoft Word, or Plain Text format
  • URLs provided for all references where available
  • Text is single-spaced, 12-point font; italics used for emphasis (not underline)
  • All figures and tables are embedded within the document (no text boxes)
  • No author identification in manuscript or document metadata (blind review)
  • Manuscript is no more than 3,000 words
  • APA citation style used throughout

Review Process

All manuscripts undergo double-blind peer review by up to three external reviewers. Reviewers and authors are anonymous to one another throughout the process.

Decisions are categorized as Accept, Revise & Resubmit, or Reject. Revised manuscripts are reviewed by a new set of external reviewers.

Acceptance rate: 53%
