Fair Share of Regular Polygon Pizzas

Humberto José Bortolossi
Humberto José Bortolossi — Fluminense Federal University
⬇ Download PDF View on legacy OJS ↗

Abstract

In his article we show how to fair share a regular polygon pizza with p sides between n people The first cut point F on the boundary is given and, as in round pizzas, the cuts are done from the center C to the boundary of the regular polygon; The Proof given is without Words using a Geogebra animation

Keywords: proof without words, fair share, regular polygons, GeoGebra
