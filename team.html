Editorial Team

The NAGJ is led by a small, dedicated editorial team committed to open-access mathematics education scholarship.

JQ
Editor
James Quinlan, Ph.D.
Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science, University of Southern Maine
Dr. Quinlan serves as Editor of the North American GeoGebra Journal. His work bridges mathematics, computer science, and technology-integrated pedagogy. He has contributed articles to the journal and brings deep familiarity with its scope and standards.
TE
Founder & Executive Editor
Michael Todd Edwards, Ph.D.
Professor of Mathematics Education, Miami University, Oxford, Ohio
Dr. Edwards founded the North American GeoGebra Journal in 2012. His professional interests include using writing as a tool to promote mathematical thinking at all levels of instruction. He is an Armstrong Professor of Education, Health, and Society and coordinates the Adolescent & Young Adult teacher education program at Miami University.
